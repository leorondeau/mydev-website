ABOUT

Before I entered into software development, I worked as a songwriter and singer, touring the US and internationally. After the world came to a stop in 2020 and the future of the music industry became uncertain, I felt the need to pivot and expand my skillset. The tech industry has always intrigued me, so I started coding and enjoyed it so much I decided to attend Nashville Software School. I've always sought new experiences and challenges that keep my mind energized with unknown possibilities. It's been great, and I'm very excited to see where it takes me!
